# Chausson

**Role:** House spirit of [[Remy Duval|Remy]]'s home; a tiny domestic Mire spirit and the party's recurring good-luck runner
**Location:** [[Sallowreach]] — Remy's house (lives in the walls / the bottom of the linen chest)
**Faction:** Independent (an honored guest)
**Tier:** —

*Improvised at the table in Session 2 and an instant table favorite. Keep him light, never deep — a sock gremlin treated with the exact grave reverence Sallowreach gives its vast unknowable spirits. That contrast is the whole joke.*

---

## What Chausson Is
A shy little Mire spirit who lives in Remy's walls and the bottom of the linen chest. He takes **one sock — never the pair** — and leaves a small "trade" in its place: a smooth pebble, a bottle cap, a single button. A polite little burglar. Nobody in the house wears socks ([[Remy Duval|Remy]]'s a barefoot frog-man), so the place is **drowning in socks Remy leaves out as courtesy** — plus neighbors gift socks "for Chausson" as a housewarming joke everyone half-means.

Local belief: a house with a sock spirit **never floods bad.** Total nonsense. Remy believes it completely.

## Remy Explains Him *(say this — delighted you asked)*
> *"Oh! That's Chausson. He's the house spirit. Lives in the walls, mostly. Bottom of the linen chest. ...You don't* see *him — nobody sees him — but you leave a sock out at night, and by morning it's gone, and there's a little* something *left in its place. Pebble. A button. Once a whole pearl, swear on my boat — well."* [his face falls about the boat for half a second, then bright again] *"ANYWAY. No no — only ever the* one *sock. Never the pair. Drives my sister mad. But that's a guest, isn't it? You don't ask a guest his business. You just leave the man some socks."* [gesturing at the piles] *"...I may have overdone the socks."*

## The "He Likes You" Beat
Whoever's been complaining about the socks reaches into a boot or pocket and finds — a single **smooth blue river-pebble,** warm, where a sock used to be. Remy gasps, genuinely moved: *"He took to you already! Oh, that's* rare. *You're in, friend. You're properly in."*

## Recurring Runner
Chausson keeps "trading" with the party all campaign — a tiny offscreen gremlin leaving pebbles and buttons, occasionally a *weirdly useful* trinket. Anyone who actually wears socks (Tenson?) is the prime target. **Never seen. Always one sock lighter.** He's the Empire's living-spell Staff's opposite number — warm, messy, alive, and asks nothing (see [[session-03-the-dinner]]).

## Key Relationships
- [[Remy Duval]] — his host, who reveres him utterly and over-provisions the socks
