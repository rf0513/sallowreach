# Madame Vivienne

**Role:** Dressmaker on the tailors' boardwalk; dresses your soul, not your body
**Location:** [[Sallowreach]] — a cramped, glorious shop on a tailors' boardwalk
**Faction:** Independent
**Tier:** 1

*Improvised in Session 2 and a table hit. A super-confident dressmaker who **does not take your input** — she reads you and tells you who you are, then makes you wear it. Four backstory mirrors and a lot of laughs. A natural recurring NPC (disguises, street rumors, embassy-fashion intel).*

---

## The Shop & Her
A cramped, glorious shop, bolts of impossible Mire-cloth to the rafters. A measuring tape round her neck she *never uses* — she eyeballs you and is never wrong. Pins in her mouth, opinions about everyone, calls people by colors (*"you — oyster-grey — in the corner"*). Theatrical, brisk, warm underneath.

> *"No no no — don't tell me what you want. People never know. I look at you, I tell you who you* are, *and then I make you* wear *it. Sit. Or stand. Turn around. ...Ah. Oh, you're* interesting."

**The hook:** she doesn't take orders, she takes *readings.* Each PC gets sized up and handed a garment that means something — the player's cue to push back, lean in, or reveal.

## Per-PC Fittings *(the fun + the mirror)*
- **🍃 Leavy** *(the best/funniest)* — a shapeshifter is a *dream* client. *"Oh, you're EASY — you can be any shape you like, so let's pick the* finest." A try-on montage; she critiques each face like outfits — then softer, the whole Witch question in one line: *"...but which one is* you, *darling? You keep changing the hanger. I can't dress a person who isn't home."* **Item:** a coat cut to drape on *any* frame she wears — *"so you needn't buy a new one every time you change your mind about being someone."*
- **🪓 Tenson** — built like a tank, nothing fits across the shoulders; she takes it as a *personal duel.* Payoff: a charcoal waistcoat with **seven inner pockets** — *"for a man who carries his work"* — and for once he looks like the respectable *clerk* he dreams of being. Quietly, secretly thrilled. *(Comedy: he winces at the price; budding-bureaucrat brain can't help itself.)*
- **🐈‍⬛ Isotta** — she **casts no reflection in the mirror.** Vivienne doesn't blink (you don't comment on a guest's nature here) — mutters, works around it, pins keep slipping off her, the cloth goes *cold.* Ends up in deep blues and silver that make her look, for once, *present.* *"There. Now they'll have to actually look at you."* *(Cat-folk gag: tail-hole, ear-room, "hold still, I'm not losing a thumb to those.")*
- **🐸 Fletcher** — a frog ranger who'd rather die than look soft. She wins him with **function:** a sharp dark coat with a hidden journal-pocket and a concealed sheath — *"a gentleman of your profession needn't go* unarmed, *only un-obvious."* **Phobia gag:** she offers a fashionable **snakeskin belt** → he recoils → she swaps to eel-leather without missing a beat: *"...noted."*

## The Twist That Makes It Matter *(anti-Caius seed)*
She clocks *where* they're going. *"Dining with the imperial man tonight? Hmf."* She dresses them in **Sallowreach** finery on purpose, not imperial fashion: *"You'll not go looking like you're hoping he* likes *you. You'll go looking like this city already decided you're* hers. *Big difference, darling. He'll feel it."* — armor of belonging, quietly anti-[[Caius Maren|Caius]]. *(Pays off at [[session-03-the-dinner|the dinner]].)*

## They're Broke — Pick One, Don't Stall
- **Credit/rep:** *"You're the ones the swamp gave back. Your coin's no good to me yet — pay when the Mire lets you. And it will. One way or another."* *(warm, faintly ominous)*
- **Barter for RP:** *"Tell me something* true *about yourselves and we'll call it square."* *(each PC pays in a confession = backstory)*
- **Remy sent word:** a bolt already set aside — *"He said you'd come. Said feed them and dress them. So."*

## Fabric Flavor *(sprinkle)*
Lantern-silk (hems glow faint after dark), tide-cloth (shifts color like the channels), **blackwood buttons** *(GM wink: the Tree Spirit's wood — no one knows)*, eel-leather, spider-silk shawls, river mother-of-pearl.
