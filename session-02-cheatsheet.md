## 🗝️ Secrets & Clues *(reveal ~half; carry the rest)*
1. **No mark** — nothing to heal; they were *tied,* not wounded. Cure = understanding, not medicine.
2. **[[Caspar Renne]]** (museum, old quarter) is the next thread on the curse.
3. Three experts, one word: **bound.** None can name what binds.
4. The garden's glowing beds **pulse on Celebration nights** — Orna never wrote it down. *(Plant.)*
5. There's a **spirit world**; the veil's thin here; small spirits pass as human.
6. The **veil's been straining for months** — more crossings, spirits slipping.
7. **Caius has been asking about the shipwreck** all over town. *(Hum. Meaning comes later.)*
8. The **SW wall is losing**; informal logbook ≠ filed reports. Someone's smoothing numbers.
9. Someone's **poaching the deep Mire to ruin** ([[adversaries/gault|Gault]]); the pipeline stages through the city.
10. **PC-personal — Tenson LIVE:** [[Magistra Silence]] told the ship was lost, won't believe it; her first letter is coming. ⟦Fletcher/Isotta: drop names.⟧

## 🚫 Hard guardrails
- **Never connect the curse to the Celebration.** Delphine stops herself; Orna/Vael say *bound* but can't name it. Garden beds & lantern-echo = **planted, not explained.** Recognition is *earned* later.
- **No marks.** Purely relational. *(Supersedes the [[sallowreach-frame|frame]]'s old "marks" lines.)*
- **The warmth is real, not a trick.** Devotion is sincere. Hand out **The Table That Adopts You** (1d4 Stress / Hope) freely.
- **Let them split; trust is slow.** Not friends yet — let them be *surprised* to start caring.

## 🎯 Win Condition for Tonight
They **understand the curse** (real, relational, uncurable by the obvious means, a thread to pull), the **Empire's hand is warmly on their shoulder,** and **four people in four rooms have started to matter.** Pace for **belonging, not plot.**
