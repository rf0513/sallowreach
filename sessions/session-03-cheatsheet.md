## 🏛️ The Embassy *(don't fumble the showpiece)*
**Two movements. Wonder, THEN seams.**
- **Wonder:** heat gone, dry cool air, real grass, apple trees blossoming AND fruiting, gold butterflies, birdsong. It feels like *home.* Let them love it.
- **Seams (drip, don't dump):** butterfly = illusion (hand passes through). Apple has no smell, no crunch — fades. **No insects, no frogs, no gnats.** Birdsong loops; breeze pulses on a schedule. Three robed mages behind a hedge, eyes shut, **real sweat,** holding the whole lie aloft.
- **The point:** Sallowreach's magic is *alive* and asks nothing; the Empire's is *illusion* maintained by hidden labor. Caius doesn't see the irony — to him this IS "better infrastructure." A clue about the Empire, filed not underlined.
