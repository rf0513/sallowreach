# Session 1 — Art Assets (Maps & Tokens)

*A clickable shopping list of **human-made** art for [[session-01-prep|Session 1 — Black Water]]. **No AI-generated art anywhere in this file** — the table hates it, and every source below is hand-drawn or hand-painted by a named artist/studio. Organized by the session's beats (see the Run Sheet in [[session-01-prep]]). Open in Obsidian preview and click straight through.*

> **Legend:** ⭐ = unusually good fit for our specific scene · **FREE** = free download/tier · 💰 = paid · 🎨 = real artist's portfolio (personal/home-game display only — credit them, don't redistribute)

> **Two kinds of art in here:** **🖼️ Vibe art** = a single pretty image to anchor attention during a *non-combat* beat (tavern, market, healer, the punt-ride). No grid, no tokens — just something to look at. **🗺️ Battlemaps** = only where dice actually fly (the bank/wreck set-piece). Session 1 has *one* real fight, so most of the night is vibe art.

## 🗺️ Battlemaps

*Session 1 has exactly **one** real fight, so you only strictly need one gridded map: the bank/wreck. (The swim, Beats 5–7, runs as theatre-of-mind in the dark — no map.)*

### The Bank / Wreck Site — the Tessak set-piece (Beats 5–9)
*The one map of the night that earns a grid — give it the good one.*
- ⭐ **[The MAD Cartographer — Swamps Map Pack (Foundry)](https://foundryvtt.com/packages/mad-swamps)**
  Includes a hand-painted "Swampy Shipwreck" — capsized galleon washed into the swamp. Exactly our wreck-on-the-bank.
- **FREE — [2-Minute Tabletop: Swamp Trail](https://2minutetabletop.com/swamp-trail-battle-map/)**
  Reeds, roots, islands; ideal muddy-bank-with-mangroves if you want bank *without* a big ship hull.
- **FREE — [Silver Compass: Shipwreck 40×30 (day + night)](https://www.silvercompassmaps.com/shipwreck-40x30/)**
- **FREE — [2-Minute Tabletop: Shipwreck](https://2minutetabletop.com/product/shipwreck/)**

### Doubles as vibe backdrops (no grid)
*These painted maps also read beautifully as static reference art for the non-combat beats above:*
- **FREE — [Seafoot Games: Mangrove Swamp Fishing Village 40×30](https://www.seafootgames.com/free-ttrpg-battlemap-mangrove-swamp-fishing-village/)** — a stilted village on black water; great backdrop for [[Remy Duval|Remy's]] tavern / the boardwalk arrival.
- **[Tom Cartos — Into the Wilds: Swamp](https://www.tomcartos.com/into-the-wilds-swamp)** & **[Czepeku channels](https://www.czepeku.com/)** — night-variant black water for the [[The Wending|Wending]] ride.

### 🔎 Best browse tool — bookmark this
- ⭐ **[Lost Atlas](https://lostatlas.co/explore/environments/swamp)** — search engine across 5,000+ free maps; filter by "swamp," size, animated. Fastest way to find more bank/channel options. Stick to known hand-drawn cartographers to avoid AI uploads.

## 🪙 Tokens

### Tessak Scout — lizardfolk man-eater w/ harpoon → [[sallowreach-roster#Tessak Scout|roster]]
*Pick a **gaunt, predatory** variant — not a noble warrior. Don't soften the scout.*
- ⭐ **[Forgotten Adventures — Tokens](https://www.forgotten-adventures.net/product-category/tokens/)** (hand-drawn, generous **FREE tier**). [Token Bundle 6](https://www.forgotten-adventures.net/product/tokens/token-bundle-6/) includes lizardfolk.
- **FREE sample — [Yasashii Kyojin Studio: Lizardfolk](https://yasashiikyojinstudio.com/products/lizardfolk-vtt-token-sample)** — grab the leanest variant.
- **FREE — [Mistcharter Maps: Lizardfolk tokens](https://mistchartermaps.com/products/lizardfolk-tokens)** (250×250).

### Mire Gar — pale, eyeless pack-fish with sick mimic-glow → [[mire-bestiary#Mire Gar|bestiary]]
*No off-the-shelf match exists — these are ours. Best route is to recolor a pike/eel.*
- ⭐ **FREE — [2-Minute Token Editor → "fish" tag](https://tools.2minutetabletop.com/token-editor/index?tag=fish)** (hand-drawn by Ross McConnell).
  **Recolor recipe:** pale grey-white body → desaturate, drop a sickly **green-cyan glow** down the flanks → stamp out **a dozen** identical minions in minutes. Tier-1 minions, so a swarm is the point.
- **[Forgotten Adventures](https://www.forgotten-adventures.net/product-category/tokens/)** also has fish/eel tokens — pale-recolor the same way.

### Lantern Rays — wonder, never a fight → [[lantern-rays]]
*The emotional center of the cold open; you'll still want them on the map.*
- 💰 **[Hit Point Press — Aquatic Token Set](https://hitpointpress.com/products/splattered-ink-games-rpg-essential-tokens-aquatic-set-digital)** — 22 top-down, 3 colorways, includes rays.
- 💰 **[Paizo — Sea Monsters VTT Token Pack](https://store.paizo.com/sea-monsters-vtt-token-pack-download/)**
- 🎨 **Real deep-sea ray photography** — an actual bioluminescent-ray still projected for the cold open is more haunting than any token. Worth a reference hunt.

## ✨ The two bespoke pieces — Sallowreach itself & the rays

*No good off-the-shelf match because they're **ours**. AI is off the table, so the human routes:*

1. **Mood-board from real concept artists** — e.g. 🎨 **[Cameron Felt — "Swamp Stilt House" (ArtStation)](https://www.artstation.com/artwork/eJgnd3)** captures the stilt-city-over-black-water feel for an establishing image. *Personal/home-game display only — credit the artist, don't redistribute.*
2. **Commission** — a single [[The Outer Market|Sallowreach]] establishing splash + a [[lantern-rays|lantern-ray]] token from a freelancer (ArtStation, r/HungryArtists) is cheap and the players will love that it's hand-made. The campaign is bespoke enough to deserve one real piece.
3. **Real reference photography** — bioluminescent rays / mangrove-bayou night shots, projected for the [[first-words|cold open]] and the [[session-01-read-aloud#11 · Sallowreach|Sallowreach reveal]].
