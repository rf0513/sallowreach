# Session 3 — Recap (As Played)

*What actually happened at the table. This is **canon**: the prep/run-sheet is what was planned, this is the record of what landed. Springboard for Session 4.*

---

## At the table
- **Present:** all four — **Fletcher** (Ranger), **Tenson** (Guardian/auditor), **Leavy** (Witch), **Isotta** (Bloodhunter). *Isotta returned.*
- **Shape:** split most of the day into solo/pair errands, then converged into **two evening groups** — a dinner pair and a hunt pair.
- **Tone:** RP-forward and **dice-forward** (the "more dice" fix worked — traps, the dinner, the hunt). The **owlbear cub** was the emotional gut-punch of the night; the campaign's thesis landed hard, on the point of Fletcher's own arrow.

---

## The Day — errands (party split)

- **Fletcher → Corentin the eel-smoker.** Chased the "demon" reports; learned it's nocturnal, climbs, and eats *smoked* food (not Mire fish). Left with **smoked eels** to bait a trap.
- **Isotta → the Still Cup** *(now a **cat café**)*. Met **Vael** — *former blood hunter,* white-eye ghost-slayer — who read her as **tied, not crossed** (a curse, not a haunting), and sent her to the **salt wall** to learn who she used to be. He also hinted he'll need her help, later, with **"the Domino man"** *(Basile, the slipping spirit)*. **⚠️ The salt wall did NOT happen yet** — she went hunting with Fletcher instead. It's her live, pending spotlight.
- **Tenson → the training field.** Trained *privately*, got prickly when the aloof-but-fun **Sable** interrupted; kept his guard up. Sable, delighted, promised to **ambush-spar him when he least expects it.** → Then **Caspar's door:** knocked *politely,* was too courteous to force it, left **suspicious of Caspar** (who stayed barricaded and silent). *(The Counterfeit Corpse encounter is still ahead.)*
- **Leavy → Orna's** *(the Verdant Margin)*. Brewed **party potions for the dinner** with Orna's chaotic genius help. Orna asked her to fetch **gloamcap distillate** from the Alchemists' Consortium (the "Persona Non Grata" quest) — and dangled the carrot: **help me, and I'll help you get your own familiar.**

## The Evening — the split

### 🍷 Dinner pair: Tenson + Leavy → the Embassy & Caius
- Stepped into the **illusion garden**; **Mr. Bigsby** (living-spell attendant) gave the tour.
- **Caius** — warm, hungry, delighted — interrogated them about the **wreck and explosion** with leading, lawyerly questions while **Bigsby transcribed a dry, damning incident report.**
- **Tenson stayed dodgy** — never revealed *why* the party is really in Sallowreach.
- **Tenson clocked the crack:** an Embassy this opulent and expensive to run, while **Oswin's ward funds get denied.** *(He doesn't know yet that Caius signed the denial — but the thread is pulled.)*
- Caius handed over the report to review, **wants all four to sign,** bring it back signed. **Tenson has concerns with how it's written.** → **UNSIGNED.** Tenson wants to redline it (being anal is the whole vibe).

### 🏹 Hunt pair: Fletcher + Isotta → the "demon"
- Corentin's clues → Fletcher baited a **smoked-eel trap.**
- **Fletcher shot first** — loosed on the shape in the dark *before seeing what it was.* The arrow hit an **owlbear cub.** He **bandaged it and saved it.**
- Followed the trail to the cub's **nest, built inside a smuggler's boat** — a **working boat, not a wreck** — a broken cage, and a manifest reading **"owlbear — 2."** Only **one** cub was there.
- **The party is keeping the boat.** *(This solves their "we need a boat" problem.)*
- Brought the cub to **Brin.** She gave Fletcher grief for shooting first — but **overall he passed her test.** She'll hand over the **snake intel later, once everyone's rested.**
