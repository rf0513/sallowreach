## Key Decisions Made

- **The tether** = cannot leave the Mire. That's it. No party-to-party distance component (removed). They can split freely within the Mire.
- **No ticking clock.** Players should not feel rushed. Urgency comes from personal stakes (where each PC was going), not a countdown. The world still *moves* — see [[world-in-motion]] for the living fronts the GM advances, slows, or redraws based on what players do. Fronts, not a doom timer.
- **Personal tether manifestations** — held in reserve until PCs are known.
- **Remy is the guide.** The separate Guide NPC stub was redundant and has been removed.
- **The Tessak are man-eaters, not sympathetic parents** *(GM decision — session 1 prep).* The deep-channel lizardfolk clan are apex predators for whom outsiders are prey; the city pretends they aren't there (an ancient stay-out-of-the-deep-channels détente under the "honored guests" fiction). **Synthesis that preserves the thematic engine:** `tessavrak` (taking-beyond-the-season restraint) and the keystone-ray collapse plot are **retained** — predators with a sustainable-take code, *corrupted by Gault into excess.* **Re-cut:** Gault buys them not with "medicine for sick children" but an **easy-supply deal** (he stirs the channels / the wrecks feed the clan — ties them to the central sabotage engine and to the party's own wreck). Session 1 gets a **Tessak Scout** (`adversaries/sallowreach-roster.md`) as the bank-beat after the gar swim. **PENDING:** full rewrite of `world/creatures/lantern-rays.md` (drop Delphine's sick-children sub-path; reframe Encounter 2 as predator diplomacy) and the Tessak bits of `world/npcs/... /gault.md` Encounter 2. Until then, roster carries a ⚠ canon note flagging the supersession.
- **The boat / rental economy is the Act 1 money engine.** Remy's boat was destroyed in the (sabotaged) wreck; until the party buys their own, they rent at [[The Mooring]] — an ongoing coin-bleed that gives gold a real destination. Boat = Bags-scale (used skiff ~1 Bag, proper Deep-Mire boat ~2 Bags); bleed = coins/handfuls. Funded via the Théo/Marcel/Caius job fork. Deep Mire access is gated behind owning the proper boat. See [[The Mooring]].
