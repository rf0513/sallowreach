## Quick Faction Map

- **Marcel** has the spear → wants **Oswin** disgraced
- **Caspar** knows the spear's significance → won't say **Marcel's** name
- **Maren** denied **Oswin's** budget → **Marcel** knows **Maren** arranged the shipwreck
- **Théo** is **Maren's** cultivated ally → doesn't know the cost of imperial "integration"
- **Adaline** keeps the Celebration → **Fen's** music breaks it
- **Delphine** → **Caspar** referral chain → leads party to the spear

**PC quests → the same engine:**
- **Fletcher's** snake → SW deep channels → the Mire's hot quadrant (wall, spirit-pressure, route to the Heart)
- **Witch's** transformation potion → the charged water → the **Tree Spirit's** essence (his cure ≈ the central force)
- **Isotta's** ghost cure → a parley with the source → the **binding** (downstream of the central choice, like the tether)
- **Tenson's** audit → **Sable's** paper + **Remy's** water → **Oswin's** embezzlement + **Caius's** sabotage
