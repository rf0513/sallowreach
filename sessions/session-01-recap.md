# Session 1 — Recap (As Played)

*The as-played record of the campaign's opening. **Canon.** The prep/read-aloud/cheatsheet were the plan; this is what landed. Springboard into [[session-02-recap]].*

## Previously, on Sallowreach *(table-ready "previously on" — read it aloud)*

> Last week, our party arrived at Sallowreach as intended — but the journey was most unconventional.
> After an unforeseen delay at the port, you boarded **The Ridgeback**, [[Remy Duval|Remy]]'s pride and joy. You admired how the brackish waters of the Mire turned still and glowed in magical beauty, and pondered the causes and effects of the town's "Celebration." Suddenly — **BLAM!** — a mysterious explosion wrecked the boat and sent you all into an unintended swim. As you fell deep into the water, you felt a *wrongness* take hold of the deepest parts of yourself.
> The waters were fast and chaotic. Some of you handled it gracefully; others were at serious risk of drowning. Thankfully, some A+ teamwork got you all to shore safely. As you made camp for the night, **two lizardfolk barbarians** tried to make you their midnight snack. You fought them off bravely — and found **Remy tied up in their rowboat,** which became the vessel that carried you the rest of the way to Sallowreach.
> Once in town, Remy took you to the local healer, **[[Delphine]]**, where she revealed a shocking truth: *you are cursed.*
> Tomorrow — you learn more about this curse, and you're free to explore the city you now call home.

---

## The Beats (canon)

- **The delay & The Ridgeback.** An unforeseen delay at the port, then aboard **The Ridgeback** — Remy's beloved boat. The party watched the Mire glow and wondered aloud about the Celebration *(first, unknowing brush with the campaign's central mystery)*.
- **The explosion.** A **mysterious blast** wrecked The Ridgeback. *(GM truth, unknown to the PCs: this was [[Caius Maren|Caius]]'s sabotage — a manufactured incident meant to leave no survivors. See [[caius-maren]] / Front 6 in [[world-in-motion]].)*
- **The wrongness in the deep.** As they sank, each felt something take hold of *the deepest part of themselves* — **the tether being laid.** The black water tied them to the Mire. They didn't know what it was yet.
- **The swim.** Fast, chaotic water. A real drowning-risk sequence; some PCs handled it, others nearly went under — **teamwork** hauled everyone to the bank.
- **The Tessak ambush.** Making camp, **two [[Tessak]] barbarians** came for them as prey — *man-eaters, played straight* (the recut in action; see STATUS "Key Decisions"). The party fought them off.
- **Rescuing Remy.** They found **Remy tied up in the Tessak rowboat** — the predators had taken him too. Freed, he guided them the rest of the way, and the **rowboat** became their ride into town.
- **Delphine's reveal.** Remy brought them to the healer **[[Delphine]]**, who examined them and named it: they are **cursed.** *(The details — that it binds them to the Mire — land in [[session-02-recap|Session 2]].)*

## Canon established this session
- ⛵ **The Ridgeback** — Remy's pride and joy, **destroyed** in the sabotaged wreck. *(Kicks off the boat-fund thread — they're boatless until the smuggler's boat in [[session-03-recap|Session 3]]. See [[The Mooring]].)*
- 🦎 **The Tessak are man-eaters** — confirmed *in play* as predators who see the warm-blooded peoples as prey. The old sympathetic framing is retired.
- 🕳️ **The tether was laid in the black water** — felt as a "wrongness" during the drowning, before anyone had a name for it.
- 🐸 **Remy survived** (taken by the Tessak, rescued by the party) — the party's first ally and guide.
