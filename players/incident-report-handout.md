## IMPERIAL INCIDENT REPORT — MIRE WATERWAYS
### Filed: Office of the Cultural Liaison, Sallowreach
### Transcription: Attendant 7 ("Bigsby") · Attested by witnesses below

**Nature of incident:** Total loss of an unregistered river vessel, southwest Mire channels. Four (4) survivors; survival attributed to chance.

**Findings, per witness testimony:**

1. The vessel operated on an **uncharted route.** No navigational markers, buoys, or lights were present or expected.

2. Passage relied **solely on the personal memory of an unlicensed guide.** No maps, no instruments, no official record of the route existed.

3. The vessel was **destroyed by explosion.** No inspection, licensing, or maintenance record can be produced. Witnesses could name **no responsible operator.**

4. **No civic or harbor authority responded** to the incident. No rescue, watch, or official presence was available. Witnesses confirm survival was owed to **luck and the guide alone.**

5. Witnesses affirm: this waterway "**nearly killed four people, with no party responsible for preventing it.**"

**Assessment:** The Sallowreach Mire remains an **ungoverned and materially unsafe** corridor for imperial-adjacent travelers. This office recommends the matter be forwarded for review of **infrastructure and oversight provisions.**

*We, the undersigned, attest the foregoing account to be true.*

## 🔍 For Tenson (GM reference — what an auditor's eye catches)
The account is *technically* built from their words, but every human truth has been edited out and every phrase bent one direction:
- **"Sabotage" appears nowhere** — the deliberate explosion is softened to *"explosion… no responsible party,"* reframing a targeted attack as generic swamp lawlessness.
- **The guide's *skill* is erased** — Remy's expertise becomes *"reliance on personal memory,"* i.e. recklessness.
- **The city's people are erased** — the warmth, the rescue, the welcome: gone. It reads as if Sallowreach abandoned them.
- **Every finding is an indictment of the *place,*** never of the person who ordered the hit.
- Leading conclusions the party never actually stated (*"ungoverned," "no party responsible"*) are attributed to their "testimony."

🎲 A re-read with an auditor's eye (*Instinct/Knowledge 13*) clocks that the report is **steered** — a document written to reach a conclusion, not to record what happened. Tenson wanting to redline it is exactly right; let him fight for every edit (the whole character).
