# Czepeku Scenes — Campaign Download List

*The "go crazy" scene haul. **Scene art = a cinematic image to show players during talky / non-combat beats** (see the battlemap-vs-scene split in [[session-01-art-assets]]). All hand-drawn — no AI. Every title below was confirmed live in Czepeku's [Scenes catalog](https://www.czepeku.com/fantasy/scenes) / [interior store](https://www.czepeku.com/store/scenes/interior).*

> **💸 The plan:** individual scenes are **$4.99 each** — for a haul this size, **subscribe one month, bulk-download everything on this list, then pause.** Their whole back-catalog is yours while active. One month buys a permanent scene library for the campaign.
>
> **🌙 Variant rule:** Sallowreach is a **dark, lantern-lit bayou**. When a scene offers lighting variants, grab **Night / Mist / Rain / Haunted / lantern-lit** over Day. Match the mood.
