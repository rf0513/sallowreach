## Delphine — the private divinations
She did **not** explain the full curse yet. The PCs already knew they were cursed; not what it does. She gave each a personal thread:

- **Ranger (Fletcher):** the snake **knows it's being hunted** — it's aware a ranger is on its trail. *"Don't let the hunter become the hunted."* → the amphisbaena is now a thinking adversary, not just quarry.
- **Guardian (Tenson):** **someone has been told the guardian is dead — and they're wrong.** That's all he knows, and it unsettles him; he suspects someone from back home. *(GM truth, not yet known to the PC: the saboteur is **Caius** — he **blew up the party's boat**. He was told it worked and they'd died; then they turned up in town alive and bloodied, so he knows he failed. The **dinner invitation is his move on the survivors.** Tenson doesn't connect any of this to Caius — he suspects someone from back home.)*
- **Witch (Leavy):** **her memories were wiped.** She had a whole life — a person with a past — before becoming the apparent automaton she is now. She remembers none of it.

---

## The personal-quest visits
- **Guardian → Sinope.** Sinope questioned him on several things and tipped him off to **the casino** — a new hook.
- **Witch (Leavy) → Orna** at the Verdant Margin. They clicked. Orna dropped backstory clues — including that **a witch needs a coven** — and Leavy has none, because she doesn't remember her past. *(Dovetails with Delphine's "memories wiped" reveal and the earlier "you're **bound**" breadcrumb.)*
- **Ranger (Fletcher) → the Hunters' Guild (Brin).** Brin **tested his motives** — accused him of hunting only for treasure. He answered that he hunts to **remove a threat, not for glory or profit** — and **passed.** Reward: a **quest to deal with a creature loose in town** (the "Haunt of Thornside" — full design in [[brin-sevarre]]).

---

## The closing revelation — what the curse DOES
Delphine ended the night by naming the curse: **it binds them to the Mire.** They **cannot leave** — step beyond the Mire and they **die.** Every one of them carries it.

*(This is the payoff the whole night was seeding: both Delphine and Orna already said "**bound**." The party's question is no longer "what's wrong with us?" but "how do we break a binding that kills us if we test it?")*

- **The boat explosion is unclaimed bait.** The wreck was flagged "mysterious and strange," but the players haven't bitten — and it was **Caius's sabotage.** Keep it on a slow fuse and let clues *intrude* rather than waiting for them to go digging: the dock gossip in [[wandering-sights]] (*"the imperial one… bought a whole table breakfast just to ask about that wreck"*) is Caius covering his tracks, and **the dinner** is the natural place to make it personal.
