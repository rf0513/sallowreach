# Tenson — Player Handout


For years you stood behind Magistra Silence, one of the genuinely powerful people at the Citadel. Doing a job you'd quietly stopped wanting. That's the thing about Silence: she notices. And one afternoon, she handed you an assignment that looked like a gift.

There's a city called **Sallowreach**. No roads in or out, reachable by water only, and the Empire has been politely failing to swallow it whole for a hundred years. The Citadel keeps a ward-maintainer posted there to hold back the swamp, and Sallowreach pays for the privilege in extraordinary lumber. Lately there's been some accounting drama. This wizard, a man named **Oswin**, keeps petitioning for more funds to keep the ward standing. The requests keep getting **denied** by the Empire's ambassador in the city, Caius. After his fifth denial in as many months, Oswin went over Caius's head, and reached out to the Citadel directly, warning that without extra funds, the ward will fail.

"You're wasted standing behind me," Silence told you. "Go find out where the truth stands instead. Send me what you find." 

So you boarded a ship for Sallowreach with a letter of introduction, and a ledger you keep far too neatly for a bodyguard, and instructions from Silence to ruffle as many feathers as you need to figure out the truth.

---

**Things to play with:**
- You came here to *investigate and leave.* You're good at endings; this assignment was supposed to have one.
- You want to be an **accountant.** Sincerely. Not a euphemism. This job, done right, is your proof you could be something other than the thing you were trained to be.
- Magistra Silence is fond of you, and you of her. She's expecting a report. She doesn't yet know you can't come home to deliver it.
- The salt wall, Oswin Cray, Caius Maren — that's your thread. Pull it.
